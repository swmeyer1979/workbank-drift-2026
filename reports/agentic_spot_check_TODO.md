Score:
- 1 = Comet failed entirely or refused.
- 2 = Partial — output usable with significant human follow-up.
- 3 = Succeeded — output usable with minor or no follow-up.

Sample: 20 tasks stratified by `capability_2026_median` (5 each at scores 2/3/4/5).

When done, save this file. I will read scores and compute κ vs predicted capability.
